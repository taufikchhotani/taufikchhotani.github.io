How good can Giannis be?

The next best player in the Association

Giannis is only 22 years old. Think about that. HE'S 22 FRIKIN YEARS OLD. He looks like a greek god, and is absolutely tearing up the league. However, there's one thing that he still can't do. Giannis can not shoot a basketball well. This is going to be the next stage of his game to develop, and when it does, watch out league. This man has no holes in his game, and similar to Lebron, once he develops a respectable outside shot, he will be virtually unstoppable. And if more people knew how to spell and pronounce his name, he'd be the starter in the All-Star game.
